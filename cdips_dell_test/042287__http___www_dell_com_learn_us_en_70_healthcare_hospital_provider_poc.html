Products that empower caregivers

Creating an environment that empowers the caregiver fosters better care for patients. That’s why Dell offers a range of products from our partners that make healthcare IT more convenient and accessible. These point-of-care and computerized provider order entry accessories enhance clinician productivity and add to patient satisfaction.

Whether you need to clear the clutter, improve security, become more mobile, ensure your power solutions meet regulatory requirements or view diagnostic images at the point of care, Dell partners have products that make your job easier.
  • Integrated Solutions
  • Resources
  • Partners

  1. Medical carts and wall mounted solutions

    Mobile carts and space-saving wall mounts can help caregivers configure their space to be more efficient.

  2. Medical Displays

    Dell partners offer an extensive line of high precision displays systems that allow you to view important diagnostic images at the point of care.

  3. Dell Thin Clients

    In a virtual desktop environment, thin clients can reduce power requirements and extend mobile cart battery life without sacrificing functionality.

  4. Mobile Computing Devices

    Rugged, medical grade tablets save time, reduce errors and ensure that the most up-to-date information stays at clinicians’ fingertips throughout all stages of care delivery.

  5. Barcode scanners and printers

    Barcode scanners ensure data is captured accurately and patients are positively identified at the bedside, improving patient safety and patient tracking.Pairing it with a mobile printer allows to print lab speciment labels at bedside.

  6. Healthcare purpose-built accessories

    Dell partners offer a range of products to help you meet compliance standards; minimize the spread of germs; meet healthcare facility codes; and help your clinicians quickly navigate complex menus through touch.

  1. Barco

    Barco offers the widest range of FDA mammography approved display solutions in the industry all backed by our industry leading 5-year warranty.
